About Me

I am a double Master's degree student in pure and applied mathematics at TUM and EPFL, respectively. My research interests are stochastic analysis, operator theory, and mathematical physics.

I hold two Bachelor degrees: one in Engineering Science, and another one in Mathematics, both from TUM.

My hobbies include jogging, biking, swimming, reading, playing the piano, and others. Some of my songs can be found below (Spotify).
