Common Questions Before Teams Start

  • Do we need to replace our existing tools?

    No. We design automation around your current stack first and only recommend tool changes when there is a clear business case.

  • How quickly can we go live?

    Most teams start with one pilot workflow in 2-4 weeks, then expand after results and controls are validated.

  • How do you prevent risky AI behavior?

    We define risk tiers, restrict AI to bounded steps, and enforce role-based approval gates on high-impact actions.

  • Who manages the automation after launch?

    We can support your team with monitoring and optimization, or hand off with documentation, training, and operating playbooks.
