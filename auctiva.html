What We Automate

Without AI: Operational Automation

Rule-based workflows for data movement, status tracking, reminders, approvals, and system sync. Ideal for stable repetitive tasks where predictability and consistency are the priority.

With AI: Assisted Decision Workflows

AI-supported classification, drafting, triage, and retrieval workflows with explicit confidence checks and human validation before high-impact actions are finalized.
